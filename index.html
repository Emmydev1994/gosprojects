SOME AREAS WHERE WE CAN HELP:

We can design your unique HR Policy Document(s):

These documents will be developed to reflect your unique organizational culture. It will be a reference document that portrays the vision and mission of your organization, engender employee engagement and foster Organizational Citizen Behavior.

We can help you standardize Employment Contracts in compliance with Nigerian Law:

Following from the Policy Document, all employment contracts will be designed reflect key policy areas that concern Staff employment. Employment Contracts and Employment letters will be separate.

We can help map your Staff Career Path:

To aid in workforce planning, employee development and retention, job classifications and careers will be mapped to streamline all employee development and career progression. This helps to promote equity and serve as a guide for developing structured learning and performance initiatives.

We can help conduct your Recruitments and Selection initiatives:

Workforce plans will often result in recruitment to fill existing staffing gaps. When the policy is developed, recruitment will then focus on matching qualification, competence and experiences with the right job. It will start by ensuring Job evaluations outcomes are captured in Job descriptions, which in turn accurately capture the desired job objective of putting the right person in the right position whilst also considering organizational fit .

We can help develop and Standardize Performance Review Processes:

help develop performance measures. The measurement of performance is very key to both employee and employer. An organizations strategic goals will form the basis for developing each employees performance contract. These contracts will further form the basis for a regular performance appraisal. The results of which will be used in deciding career progression including rewards and retention strategies.
